About The Assembly

Upholding Democracy, Serving the People of Edo State.

Our Mission

To make laws for the peace, order, and good governance of Edo State, and to effectively represent the interests of our constituents through robust oversight and legislative excellence.

Our Vision

To be a model legislature in Nigeria, distinguished by its transparency, accountability, and unwavering commitment to the sustainable development and prosperity of all people in Edo State.

Structure of the House

The Edo State House of Assembly is a unicameral legislature composed of 24 members, each elected from a state constituency. The House is led by the Speaker, who is the presiding officer, assisted by the Deputy Speaker.

The administrative wing is headed by the Clerk of the House, who oversees the day-to-day operations and staff. The legislative work is primarily done through a system of Standing Committees, which are responsible for specific areas of government (e.g., Finance, Education, Health) and perform oversight functions.
